## Tryouts

The tryouts for _parallelized_ will be held in two phases.

**Phase 1:** A small form to gauge your skill level and experience with Python. Please fill out the [form](https://ls.parallelized.in/tsrsm-2023-tryout) as soon as you can.

**Phase 2:** A coding round to test your Python skills. This will be held online (through Discord) on **Wednesday, October 4, 2023**. More details about timings and such will be added to this page and will be communicated on the [Discord server](https://ls.parallelized.in/discord) (please make sure to join).
